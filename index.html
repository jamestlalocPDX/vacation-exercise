Why vacation here?

It's green AF. It has all four seasons. Them rolling hills though.

What do you do in Austria?

You can ski. You can swim. You can hike. Sit on your porch. Sing in the hills.

How do you get to Austria?

You can fly, train, and drive! You can wave at the birds passing by! The hills are, in fact, alive!

Things you don't do in Austria:

  • Litter
  • Point your finger
  • Rob people
  • Fight its citizens, unless they started it!
  • Litter, seriously, don't do this anywhere.
